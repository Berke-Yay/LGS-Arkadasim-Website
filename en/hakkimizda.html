About Us

Initiated from Koç High School and established in 2021, LGS Arkadaşım(My LGS Friend) is a project that aims to help 8th graders all around Turkey to reach their full potentials in their LGS(High School Entrance Exam) journey via offering students a free of cost one-to-one mentorship and lots of exam practices.

What Seperates Us from Other Programs?

A Qualified Team

Mentors in our team are selected after a careful process of interviews from outstanding high schools of Turkey such as Koç High School, Galatasaray High School and Robert College.

Mentorship

Each student has their own mentor. Mentors contact their students on a regular basis, and they answer the students' questions, giving them some advice. Mentors also help their students outside of academics.

Exams

There is an exam every week and the mentors give homework to their students based on the exam analysis.

Lessons in small classes

A separate class is opened for each subject, the students send the questions they cannot do, and these questions are solved in a small class on Zoom in weekly lessons.

Support on Decision Period

Mentors keep guiding their students after the exam, helping them to choose the right high school for their interests and goals.

History and Future Plans

  1. 2021 Under the leadership of Ayşesu Özdoğan, the program was established with a few friends of her from Koç High School.
  2. 2022 After its first year, the program has grown within The Koç High School, and therefore the number of students and mentors has increased significantly compared to the first year.
  3. 2023 Our bright students in the program this year have achieved many successes. Defne Demirci, one of our students, is one of the 562 students in Turkey and got 500 points in the exam. She will study at Koç High School with a full scholarship next year.
  4. 2024 Although we have been receiving mentors only from The Koç High School for the last two years, our plan for next year is to receive applications from other successful high schools in Istanbul. Also, unlike in previous years, we plan to take our students from all over Turkey.

About Us

Who Are We?

LGS My Friend project is a Koç High School based project established in 2021. We have laid the foundations of the program in the first two years and started narrowly, but we plan to receive applications from all over Turkey next year. The program was founded by Ayşesu Özdoğan and now Ömer Öztok is continuing it.

After the mentor selection made every year, we identify the students who are selected through application and who have scholarships from public or private schools. Right after we accept them to the program, we provide one-to-one matches with the mentors. The student is called and contacted by his mentor every week. In addition, by looking at the analysis from the exams every week, the mentor determines the student's lack of topic and gives homework about that. We put these students in classes according to the courses they want to take. The mentors solve the LGS questions they send to the mentors in small lesson groups over Zoom. In addition, the program provides books for the students.

What are We Doing?

What separates us from the other projects?

Mentorship

Each student has their own mentor. Mentors help the students more than just academics. After the LGS exam, the mentors help their students to pick the right school for them.

Exams

There is an exam every week and the mentors gives homework to their students based on the exam analysis.

Lessons in small classes

A separate class is opened for each subject, the students send the questions they cannot do, and these questions are solved in a small class on Zoom in weekly lessons.
